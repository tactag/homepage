About
One dev.
Deliberate tools.

TACTAG is a solo developer brand focused on building utility-first applications for the people doing the actual work — field supervisors, operations teams, and anyone who's tired of tools that weren't made for them.

Every app under the TACTAG umbrella starts with a real problem. The goal isn't feature parity with enterprise software — it's precision. Tools that do exactly what they need to do, and nothing they don't.

Stack of choice: Node.js, Python, React, Firebase — with a strong preference for automation over repetition, and local-first over vendor lock-in.
